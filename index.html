About Me (CV)

I am currently a PhD student with Stefanos Zafeiriou in the Intelligent Behaviour Understanding Group (IBUG) at Imperial College London.

Before coming to IC, I obtained my master and bachelor degree from Nanjing Universify of Information Science and Technology. During my master study, I was with the BDAT Lab under the supervision of Prof. Qingshan Liu, and I worked closely with Prof. Dacheng Tao.
